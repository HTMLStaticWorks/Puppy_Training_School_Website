Quick Tips for Owners

Consistent Cues

Always use the exact same hand signal with your verbal command.

Keep it Short

Puppy brains tire quickly. Limit training sessions to 5-10 minutes.

High-Value Rewards

Use treats like boiled chicken for difficult new tasks like recall.
