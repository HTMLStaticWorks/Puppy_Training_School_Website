Master Class

Advanced behavioral modification & off-leash prep for the ultimate canine companion.

Program Details

The Master Class is for owners who want the absolute best connection and reliability with their dog. This 12-session intensive program moves beyond basic manners into advanced distraction proofing and off-leash foundations.

Weekly Schedule

Week 1-3: Advanced Leash Skills - handling lunging and high-prey drive distractions.
Week 4-6: Off-Leash Foundations - long line work and distance reinforcement.
Week 7-9: Distance Commands - mastering 'Drop' and 'Stay' from 50+ feet away.
Week 10-12: Public Access Mastery - calm behavior in cafes and hardware stores.
