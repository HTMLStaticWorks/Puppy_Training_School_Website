The PuppySchool Mission

Founded in 2026, we recognized a major flaw in the dog training industry: the reliance on fear and intimidation to force compliance.

We believe dogs are family members who deserve to be taught with patience, clarity, and rewards. Our mission is to equip dog owners with the communication tools they need to build an unbreakable bond of trust and mutual respect.
