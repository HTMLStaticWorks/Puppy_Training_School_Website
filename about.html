Who We Are

Who We Are

We are certified dog behaviorists dedicated to creating harmonious households through positive, science-based training.

The PuppySchool Mission

Founded in 2026, we recognized a major flaw in the dog training industry: the reliance on fear and intimidation to force compliance.

We believe dogs are family members who deserve to be taught with patience, clarity, and rewards. Our mission is to equip dog owners with the communication tools they need to build an unbreakable bond of trust and mutual respect.

The Core Values That Drive Us

Positive Reinforcement

We never use fear, pain, or intimidation. We focus on rewarding wanted behaviors to build confidence and joy in learning.

Expert Guidance

Our team consists of certified professional dog trainers (CPDT-KA) who stay current with the latest behavioral science.

Family-Centric Approach

We don't just train dogs; we coach humans. We ensure the whole family knows how to communicate with their furry member.

Trusted by 2,000+ Local Families

Since our inception, we've helped thousands of puppies transition from chaotic bundles of energy into well-mannered, happy companions. Our methods are endorsed by local veterinarians and rescue organizations across the city.
