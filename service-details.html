House Training & Potty Routines

End indoor accidents with our proven positive reinforcement methodology.

Overview

House training is the most critical first step for any new puppy owner. Indoor accidents are stressful for you and confusing for your puppy if not handled correctly. Our comprehensive method removes the guesswork, establishing a clear schedule and communication system.

What You Will Learn

  • Reading Signals: Learn to spot the subtle cues your puppy gives before they need to go.
  • Schedule Creation: Establish a bio-rhythm sync that works for both your dog and your daily routine.
  • Crate Training: Proper, humane use of a crate as a safe den and training tool, not a punishment.
  • Accident Cleanup: How to properly neutralize odors so your puppy isn't drawn back to the same spot.
