Why is My Puppy Biting Everything?

Sarah Miller
Master Trainer • Oct 05, 2026 • 5 min read

Puppy biting, or mouthing, is completely normal behavior. Puppies use their mouths to explore the world, and during teething, their gums are often sore. However, teaching them bite inhibition is crucial.

Redirection is Key

Instead of pulling your hand away and squealing (which can look like play to a puppy), offer them a toy immediately. Reward them when they choose the toy over your fingers.

Time Outs

If the puppy gets too excited and won't stop biting, a 30-second 'reverse time out' where you leave the room can teach them that biting ends the social interaction.
