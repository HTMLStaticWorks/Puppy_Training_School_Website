Confidence

Anxiety Solutions

Gentle methods to build confidence and stop panic when left alone.

Overview

Separation anxiety is heartbreaking for both the dog and the owner. We focus on low-stress, gradual training that helps your dog learn to be calm and relaxed when home alone, eliminating destructive behavior, excessive barking, and panic.

What You Will Learn

  • Alone-Time Training: Building up your dog's tolerance for solitude in very small, stress-free increments.
  • Settle Commands: Teaching your dog how to choose relaxation on their bed or in their crate.
  • Counter-Conditioning: Changing your dog's emotional response to your departure cues (keys, shoes, etc.).
  • Mental Stimulation: Using puzzles and games to keep your dog's mind occupied and reduce stress.
