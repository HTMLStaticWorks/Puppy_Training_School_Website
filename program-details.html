Core Obedience Program

Our most popular 8-session package designed to transform your dog into a perfectly mannered companion.

Program Details

The Core Obedience Program spans 8 weeks, with one 60-minute in-home session per week. We focus heavily on reinforcing fundamental commands under increasing levels of distraction. This program is suitable for dogs 4 months and older who have outgrown the basic puppy phase but need structured discipline.

Weekly Schedule

Week 1-2: Solidifying Sit, Down, and Watch Me in a low distraction environment.
Week 3-4: Introducing Stay and Leave It with moderate household distractions.
Week 5-6: Loose leash walking in the neighborhood, desensitizing to triggers.
Week 7-8: Reliable Recall (Come) and public access manners at local parks.
